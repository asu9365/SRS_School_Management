# SchoolOS - Digital School Ecosystem

## Project Master Plan & Roadmap

---

# Project Overview

SchoolOS is a next-generation Student-Centric Digital School Ecosystem designed for St. Robert's High School (SRHS).

The platform extends beyond traditional School Management Systems by providing a unified ecosystem connecting:

* Students
* Parents
* Teachers
* Principal
* Administration
* Counselors

The objective is to eliminate communication gaps between school and parents while creating a complete digital academic journey for every student.

---

# Vision Statement

> Every student's academic, behavioral, co-curricular, and personal growth journey should be measurable, visible, and collaboratively managed by teachers, parents, and school administration.

# Long-Term Vision

SchoolOS should evolve from a single-school portal into a multi-tenant SaaS platform capable of serving multiple schools with:

* Dedicated School Portals
* Separate Databases
* Custom Branding
* Subscription Plans

The final product should function as a complete School Operating System rather than a traditional School Management System.
